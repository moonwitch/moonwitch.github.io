## MacOS

I have been using MacOS for a while, namely since late 2011. At work, I was given a Macbook Pro then.

![Neofetch-MacOS]({{site.baseurl}}/assets/img/posts/neofetch-mac.png)

I've - obviously - upgraded the RAM from 4GB to 8GB and I've replaced the HDD with a Solid State of the same size; 500GB. You can see, from the NeoFetch screenshot, it's actually not that bad for being 8 yrs old. And admittedly, outside of poor battery this old Macbook still performs quite well. It has served me well over the years, although I now don't use it as often. I've tried to install Linux (Manjaro or Ubuntu) on it, but the dual graphics wreck the experience every single time.
